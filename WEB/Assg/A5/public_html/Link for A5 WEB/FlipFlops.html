Flip Flops

These don't really warrant much of an explanation. The most practical pair of shoes every woman must own, flip-flops are ideal everyday sandals, comprising of a flat sole.
These shoes for women feature a Y-shaped strap starting from the front of the sole and ending at the sides. There's no dearth of flip-flop designs in the market, encompassing everything between regular rubber to glittery metallic straps.
